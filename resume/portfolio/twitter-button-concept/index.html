Interaction Notes

Based on Erik Deiner's Dribbble concept, this prototype turns a simple social button into a small physical object.

  • Notice the cover, spine, and shadow layers working together to imply depth.
  • The reveal works on hover and focus-within, so keyboard users can still reach the link.
  • The demo stays intentionally constrained because the whole idea lives in one small moment.
