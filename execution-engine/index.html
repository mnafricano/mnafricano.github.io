Design principles

Built for inconsistent activation.

01

Externalize the invisible

If the task lives only in your head, it can mutate forever. The first win is making it visible.

02

Shrink the door

The starting action should feel almost too small to respect. That is the point.

03

Reward contact

Progress begins when you touch the task. Finishing is excellent, but contact is the habit.
