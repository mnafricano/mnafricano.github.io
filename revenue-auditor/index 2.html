Methodology & limitations

Transparent rules, cautious estimates.

How are findings calculated?

The auditor uses deterministic comparisons: time without invoice references, invoice values versus confirmed rates, outstanding balances after matched payments, retainers versus current billing, anniversary pricing, contract dates, and monthly hours versus included scope. Every finding exposes its arithmetic and evidence.

What can it not determine?

It cannot interpret side letters, credits, verbal agreements, tax treatment, or accounting policy. Image-only PDFs need OCR and are not supported. Currency conversion is not performed; use one currency per audit. Review every result against your source systems.

Where is my data stored?

Only in IndexedDB in this browser profile. Clearing site data can erase it. Export reports you need to retain. No analytics or user-data network requests are made.
